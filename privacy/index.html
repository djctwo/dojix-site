Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Dojix ("the App") is operated by Aaxis Holdings Group LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. We built Dojix with a privacy-first architecture: your trading data stays on your Mac, under your control.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Trade data — Imported trades are stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your Mac. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
  • API keys — If you use integrations (Anthropic, Twelve Data, OANDA), your keys are stored in the macOS Keychain and never transmitted to our servers.
  • App lock password — Hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256 (100,000 iterations) and stored locally. We never see your plaintext password.
  • Subscription info — Payments processed by Paddle. We receive email, subscription status, and transaction IDs. We never see your credit card details.

Collected automatically:

  • App version — Sent during update checks via Sparkle. No personal data included.

We do NOT collect:

  • Usage analytics, telemetry, or behavioral data
  • Screen visits, feature usage, or session duration
  • Crash reports
  • Cookies, pixels, or web tracking
  • IP addresses for tracking
  • We do not serve ads or sell your data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App functionality — All analytics operate on data stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription management — Email and Paddle data verify your subscription status.
  • Software updates — Sparkle checks for and delivers updates.
  • Support — If you contact us, we use your info to resolve your issue.

3a. AI Analysis — What We Send to Anthropic

When you use AI edge detection (Pro and Pro+ plans), Dojix sends aggregated, anonymized trade statistics to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Here is exactly what is and is not sent:

Sent:

  • Win rate, profit factor, expectancy, and other aggregate metrics
  • Performance breakdowns by session, hour, day of week, and currency pair
  • R-multiple distributions and risk metrics
  • Trade count and direction summaries

Never sent:

  • Individual trade details (ticket numbers, exact entry/exit prices, timestamps)
  • Account numbers, broker names, or account balances
  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Screenshots, notes, or journal entries
  • API keys or credentials for any service

Data is transmitted directly from your Mac to Anthropic over HTTPS with certificate pinning. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of API inputs. Per Anthropic's API terms, data sent via the API is not used to train their models.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data stored in a local SQLite database on your Mac, encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM
  • API keys in macOS Keychain (hardware-backed encryption)
  • App Lock with PBKDF2-SHA256 hashing (100,000 iterations) and brute-force protection
  • All network connections use HTTPS/TLS
  • We have no ability to remotely access your data

5. Data Retention

  • Local data — Stays on your Mac until you delete it. No retention limits.
  • Subscription data — Paddle retains billing records as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

Dojix is designed for adult traders. Not intended for children under 16.

7. Your Rights

Your data is local, so you have direct control: access, export (CSV/PDF), edit, and delete at any time. For GDPR/CCPA rights related to Paddle subscription data, contact us or Paddle directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We'll update the date at the top when changes occur. Significant changes will be noted in app update notes.
