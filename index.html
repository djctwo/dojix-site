Everything you need to find your edge

From auto-import to AI recommendations, Dojix turns your trade history into actionable intelligence.

📊

53 Analytics Components

Equity curves, session heatmaps, R-multiple distributions, Monte Carlo simulations, pair correlations, drawdown recovery, and 47 more.

🤖

AI Edge Detection

Claude AI analyzes your trades and gives you ranked recommendations with dollar impact. "You make $47/trade more in London session."

📥

Auto-Import 9 Platforms

MT5, MT4, cTrader, OANDA API, TradingView, Interactive Brokers, Thinkorswim, NinjaTrader, and generic CSV.

🎯

Trade Grading A-F

Every trade scored on R-multiple, execution efficiency, timing discipline, and trade management. See your grade distribution evolve.

📈

Live Session Intelligence

Real-time forex quotes, economic calendar with danger zone alerts, currency strength bars, and edge signals.

🔒

Your Data Stays Local

Everything stored on your Mac in an encrypted Keychain. No cloud. No servers. No one sees your P&L but you.

🎲

Monte Carlo Simulation

1,000 randomized equity curves with confidence bands. Know your probability of hitting targets — or blowing up.

📋

Prop Firm Tracker

Configure account size, profit target, daily loss limit, max drawdown. Track your challenge progress in real time.

🧠

Behavioral Analytics

Discipline score across overtrading, revenge trading, tilt events, and lot size escalation. A-F grading for your behavior.

Every trade, graded A-F

Full trade journal with P&L, cumulative equity, R-multiples, session tags, and automatic grade scoring. Filter by pair, session, direction, or date range. Export to PDF or CSV.

12 metrics at a glance

Win rate, profit factor, expectancy, Sharpe ratio, Calmar ratio, drawdown, R-multiple, streaks, duration — all computed in real time. Plus a prop firm tracker for challenge accounts.

Find where you make money

Edge Report ranks your top money-makers and money-losers by session, hour, day, and pair. Dollar amounts on everything. AI analysis gives you 3-5 ranked recommendations.

Live session intelligence

Real-time session clock, economic calendar with high-impact event alerts, and a personalized edge signal telling you whether conditions match your best setups.
