## Decisions Made
- Privacy/terms pages don't get hamburger nav — too few links, "Back to Home" suffices
- Features page groups: chose to keep Timing (#5) inside "Only in Dojix" group (04-06) since it sits between the two hero showcases in DOM order
- FAQ schema limited to 6 most searchable questions rather than all 20+ (keeps structured data focused)
- SEO keyword strategy prioritizes unique Dojix features (Monte Carlo, Kill Zones) as long-tail targets since no competitors own those SERPs
