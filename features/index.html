Features

See Dojix in action

Every screen built for one purpose: helping you find and repeat your edge.

Trade Journal

Every trade logged with P&L, R-multiple, session, hour, grade, and running cumulative equity. Add notes, tags, screenshots, emotional state, and commission costs per trade.

  • Auto-graded A-F on 4 dimensions
  • Filter by pair, session, direction, date range
  • Cumulative P&L column shows your equity curve inline
  • Trade replay with play/pause and running stats
  • Export to PDF or CSV

Edge Report

Ranks your top 5 money-makers and money-losers by session, hour, day, and pair. Dollar amounts on everything. No guessing where your edge actually is.

  • AI analysis: 3-5 ranked recommendations
  • Dollar impact per recommendation
  • Bias alignment and volatility regime breakdown
  • Tag analytics for custom setup tracking
  • Post-close continuation analysis

Pulse — Live Session Intelligence

Real-time session clock with time remaining, economic calendar with high-impact event alerts, and a personalized edge signal that tells you whether current conditions match your best setups.

  • Live forex quotes from your watchlist
  • Danger zone alerts before high-impact news
  • Currency strength bars
  • Session intelligence: FOCUS / OK / REDUCE / AVOID
  • Customizable 8-pair watchlist
Analytics

53 analytics components. Zero fluff.

Every component earns its place by answering a question about your trading.

Monte Carlo Simulation

1,000 randomized equity curves with 5th/25th/median/75th/95th percentile confidence bands. Probability of hitting profit targets and drawdown thresholds.

Prop Firm Tracker

Configure account size, profit target, daily loss limit, max drawdown. Real-time progress bars. Track your FTMO, MFF, or any prop challenge.

Behavioral Analytics

Discipline score (A-F) across 4 dimensions: overtrading detection, revenge trading, tilt events, and lot size escalation after losses.

Drawdown Recovery

Time underwater chart, top 10 drawdown events by depth, recovery phase analysis comparing your behavior during drawdown vs recovery.

Exit Efficiency Analysis

Circular efficiency gauge (0-100%), money left on table, distribution by bucket, early exit detection with post-close continuation data.

Emotional P&L Tracker

Tag trades with emotional state (Calm, Confident, Anxious, Frustrated, Revenge, FOMO, Bored). See average P&L per emotion.

Checklist Compliance

Define your trading rules, check them per trade. See compliance rate, dollar impact of following vs breaking each rule, best/worst rule badges.

Playbook Catalog

Tag-based setup tracking. Each setup classified as Keep (PF ≥ 1.0), Review, or Drop. Per-setup metrics, P&L sparklines, best/worst session.

Pair Correlation Matrix

Pearson correlation between your traded pairs. Diversification insights. Are you unknowingly doubling down on correlated positions?

Risk of Ruin

Kelly criterion, risk gauge, actionable risk insights. Know your mathematical probability of blowing the account at current sizing.

Commission & Fee Tracking

Total costs, cost per trade, cost by symbol chart, cost as percentage of gross profit. Know what your broker is really charging you.
